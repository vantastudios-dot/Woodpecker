Our Story

The Woodpecker Experience

Nestled in the heart of Hazratganj at Level Zero, Hotel Charans Plaza, Woodpecker is Lucknow's beloved destination for those who seek more than just a night out. With a warm, sophisticated ambiance and an all‑day menu crafted to delight, it's the city's favourite escape.
